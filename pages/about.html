Our Mission

Education should be accessible to all. Period. This is why at Aspire Access, we strive to provide quality education to people around the world. We currently offer over 400 courses at up to 100% lower cost than any other program. Our funding for courses comes from grants provided by the US Department of Education, Google, Microsoft, and other companies. Our goal is to be able to provide free courses by utilizing the public library system to allow student to use their books for free. Together we can give ALL students the opportunity to succeed.
